Haizhou Wang is a Ph.D. student at the College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, under the supervision of [Prof. Han Yu (于涵)](https://scholar.google.com.sg/citations?user=eXgoTXMAAAAJ&hl=en). 
Prior to this, he served as a Research Assistant at Shanghai University, collaborating with [Prof. Yangguang Cui (崔阳光)](https://yangguangcui.github.io). 
He received his M.S. degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology, East China Normal University, Shanghai, China, in 2023, advised by [Prof. Tongquan Wei (魏同权)](https://scholar.google.com.sg/citations?user=yhA9e3YAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao). 
His research interests primarily focus on the Internet of Things (IoT), Federated Learning, and Dynamic Neural Networks. 
He has published several refereed papers in these areas in premium conferences and journals, including IEEE TCAD, ACM MM, IEEE IoTJ, and JSA.
